David Chandler created the Berkeley Mini Stat Mech Meeting in 2000 and oversaw its development into an annual tradition that is enjoyed and valued by a broad community of molecular scientists. The meeting’s weekend-long program has consistently drawn speakers and participants from the best statistical mechanicians in the world, highlighting areas of modern interest and contributions from emerging leaders. Programs from past meetings are provided below.
